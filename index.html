Timelyzer

Track and control your app usage time

What is Timelyzer?

Timelyzer is an Android application that helps users track how much time they spend on different apps. You can visualize your usage patterns with daily and weekly charts.

Key Features

  • Track app usage by day and week
  • Set time limits for individual apps
  • Receive alerts when time limits are exceeded
  • User-friendly and clean interface
  • No data is sent or stored externally

Privacy First

Timelyzer processes all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. When you uninstall the app, all usage data is automatically deleted.
